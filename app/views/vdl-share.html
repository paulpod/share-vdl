Share driving licence

You can share your driving licence with an employer or vehicle hire firm, for example.

You may give them the code below, plus the last 8 digits of your driving licence number to verify your details. Only current endorsements, and provisional status are shared.

The code is valid for 48 hours, and only one use.

ABC12819

You may also share a PDF of this information with them, which contains the same code to allow them to verify the details.
